New Service - Mac EC2 Instance

... Testing macOS app on AWS is now possible

Photo from AWS official blog.
AWS has released a new type of EC2 instance, Mac EC2. Accoding to its official blog, those regions with Mac EC2 available have racks of Mac mini hardwares in their data centers and can be used for developing, testing, packaging, and signing Xcode applications including macOS, iOS, tvOS,and watchOS.
reference: https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/new-use-mac-instances-to-build-test-macos-ios-ipados-tvos-and-watchos-apps/
